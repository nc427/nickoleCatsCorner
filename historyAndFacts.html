Brief History of Domesticated Cats

The earliest indication of interactions between humans and cats at around 3100 BC in Ancient Egypt. People valued the protection cats provided from killing rodents. Due to Egypt becoming a province of the Roman Empire, many cats had migrated and populated in Europe. From Europe, reportedly cats migrated via the Mayflower to the United States and made their ways into their lives as household pets to many people.

How is civilization with cats now?

Some factors that can help with narrow down your decisions can be:

  • Family: If you have kids at home, especially small children, try looking for an older cat due to the amount of time a kitten it will take to raise it.
  • Other pets: It takes a while for cats to develop a relationship to another pet in your home.
  • Living arrangements: Some cats do well in small spaces and some like to roam around and explore.

There is a plot twist when "choosing" a cat for you. Basically... the cat will choose you.
