American Shorthair

These cats come in a variety of colors.
They are patient and an overall perfect companion.

British Shorthair

Known for their rounded wide face and grey coat.
They do not have a temperamental side and are very intelligent

Sphynx

Famously known as a "hairless cat" with big ears and lemon shaped eyes.
They are fun-loving and energetic but prepare for the frequent cleaning.

Ragdoll

Have a distinct color point and blue eyes as well as a thick white coat.
They are affectionate, gentle and calm.

Scottish Fold

These cats ears fold down and have distinct flat faces with orange eyes.
They are sweet, calm, loyal and adaptable.

Siamese

Most recognized cats derived from Asia. They also have a distinct color point and shorthair coat.
They are very intelligent, sociable and often described as "dog-like".

Maine Coon

Known to be larger than average cats with thick,heavy coats and big bones.
They are friendly and overall perfect for a family.

American Longhair

Just like the American short hair... but instead have long thick hair.
They are playful but do tend to be less active than the Abyssinian cat breed.

Abyssinian

They have a long muscular body with a wedge shaped head and almond eyes.
They are talkative and very loving.

Bengal

Known for their leopard coat as well as long, athletic built and have short hair.
They are very sweet and extremely active.

Devon Rex

They have long ears, high cheekbones and often described as alien-like appearance.
These cats are fun and highly active.

Russian Blue

This cat is more on the hypoallergenic side and have a dense grey coat and blue eyes.
They are calm, affectionate (but not clingy) and smart.

Birman

Typically have a color-point, long haired silky coats and noticeable white paws.
They are gentle, easy going and curious. Perfect for a family.

Orange Tabby

They have a M-shaped marking on the forehead and pale color on the chin and tummy.
Super talkative, social and energetic.

Tortoiseshell

Known for their marbled, black and brown coats.
They can have a high-temperament and sassy personality.
