Behaviors

To say the least, cats can portray the most unpredictable set of behaviors. They are the most affectionate creature in the world to biting and scratching your arm in the span of a second.

Some Typical Behaviors include:

  • Purring: The universal sign of affection and happiness that a cat may display.
  • Grooming: Cat's tend to want to be clean and will do so by licking themselves.
  • Kneading: They turn into little bakers by stretching and retracting their paws in an alternating motion. Just make sure they're doing it on a soft surface like a pillow.
  • Climbing: They climb to the highest height that so they can proclaim themselves as the head of the household.
